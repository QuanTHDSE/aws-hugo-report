### Lessons Learned

**Technical Lessons:**

1. **Start Simple, Scale Smart**
   - Begin with simplest solution (serverless)
   - Add complexity only when needed
   - Measure and optimize based on real data
   - Over-engineering is more costly than under-engineering

2. **Infrastructure as Code is Not Optional**
   - Manual infrastructure is error-prone
   - Code enables reproducibility and disaster recovery
   - Version control infrastructure changes
   - Collaboration becomes easier with IaC
   - Enables automated testing of infrastructure

3. **Monitoring Matters More Than You Think**
   - You can't improve what you don't measure
   - Real-time visibility prevents issues
   - Historical data enables optimization
   - Alerts keep you informed, not alarmed
   - Good observability makes debugging 10x easier

**Professional Lessons:**

4. **Network is Your Biggest Asset**
   - Most opportunities come through relationships
   - Community support accelerates learning
   - Peer advice prevents costly mistakes
   - Mentors open doors
   - Collaboration multiplies productivity

5. **Vietnamese Builders Are World-Class**
   - Seeing peers build impressive solutions
   - Vietnamese talent is globally competitive
   - Local ecosystem is thriving
   - Opportunities abound for builders
   - Pride in being part of growing community

6. **Cloud Transforms More Than Technology**
   - Business models change with cloud
   - Organizations can be more agile
   - Innovation becomes faster and cheaper
   - Global competition is now local
   - Success requires mindset shift, not just tooling
