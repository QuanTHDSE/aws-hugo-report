### Lessons Learned

**Professional Lessons:**

1. **Business-First Thinking**
   - Always understand business problems first
   - Align technical decisions with business goals
   - Communicate value in business terms
   - Stakeholder buy-in is crucial for success

2. **The Power of Visual Modeling**
   - Event storming brings instant clarity
   - Collaborative modeling aligns understanding
   - Visual diagrams communicate better than documents
   - 2-3 hours of modeling saves weeks of design mistakes

3. **Community and Mentorship Matter**
   - Learning accelerates with expert guidance
   - Peer support helps overcome challenges
   - Diverse perspectives improve solutions
   - Relationships built enable ongoing collaboration
